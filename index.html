I pursue my PhD majoring in Logic and AI at University of Amsterdam, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Robert Van Rooij, cosupervised by Prof. Kun Zhang and Prof. Fenrong Liu, I am also a Visiting Ph.D. Student of Machine Learning Department at MBZUAI supervised by Prof. Mingming Gong. Before that, I obtained a Master’s degree in Logic from Tsinghua University. My research interests including Neuro-Symbolic AI, logical reasoning for LLMs and causal inference. span from causal machine learning theory, counterfactual fairness, recommender system debiasing, out-of-distribution generalization, and logical reasoning of large language models. Moreover, I am supported by the Young Scientists Fund of the National Natural Science Foundation of China (¥300,000) and Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program by CAST - Doctoral Student Special Plan (via CCF). I have been selected as the 2024 Peking University Person of the Year and representative of National Scholarship reported by People's Daily. I have served as the area chair (AC) or program committee (PC) member for top-tier conferences including ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, SIGKDD, WWW, AAAI, IJCAI, CVPR, ICCV, and the invited reviewer for prestigious journals such as TPAMI, TKDE, TOIS, TKDD, TNNLS, JASA, SCIENCE CHINA Information Sciences, and The Innovation.
